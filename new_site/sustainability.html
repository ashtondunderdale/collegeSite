Sustainability

Building Futures, Changing Lives

Vehicle Charging


Chargin ports for owners of electric cars have been constructed within the parking space of Burnley College in order to appeal to owners of these particular vehicles.

Recycling


Recycling bins have been placed all around the college campus in order to encourage people to throw away their rubbish properly and in a carbon safe way.

The Green Effect


Reusable cutlery and dishes / cups have been implemented within the college cafe as a form of carbon neutral methods.
