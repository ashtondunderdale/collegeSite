Careers within industry

Computing careers involve the design, development, and maintenance of computer systems and networks. These
professionals may work in various industries, including information technology, finance, and healthcare.

Business careers involve the management of an organization's financial resources, including marketing, sales, and
operations. These professionals may work in a variety of industries, including finance, marketing, and consulting.

Travel and hospitality careers involve the planning and coordination of travel and accommodation for individuals
and groups. These professionals may work in the tourism industry, hotels, or airlines.

Uniformed services careers involve the protection of people and property, and include roles such as law
enforcement, firefighting, and the military. These professionals are often required to undergo rigorous training
and may work in challenging and high-pressure environments.
